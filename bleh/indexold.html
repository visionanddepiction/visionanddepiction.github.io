Perception and Depiction 2024

Where: Delft University of Technology
When: 7-9 February 2024

This conference about pictorial production and perception aims to bring artist, designers and scientests together to discuss various overlapping themes. These themes are based on the Vision and Depiction framework which describes the form and conte(x/n)t of pictures. The main talk sessions will center around the 4 pictorial factors texture, light/colour, space and material. Within these themes we hope to evoke discussions on both the medium and representation but also in the directions of the referential functions such as described in semiotics and iconography and further contextual contemplation.
